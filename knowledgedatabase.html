Public health volunteers

Our public health volunteers offer answers to your questions that cannot be answered by our chatbots through email. In the event that you cannot find the answer to your questions after using our chatbots, consider emailing us. We will try our best to respond within two hours of initial contact.

Our public health volunteers are students at the University of Maryland studying a variety of health-related disciplines. They source information from the CDC, WHO, and Medline. These websites, among others, are listed in our Important Resources section. If you’d like a full overview of all of our public health defenders and what they’re studying, feel free to visit our about page.

Feedback

We welcome feedback on both our public health volunteer initiative and our chatbots. If you have questions, please email our public health volunteers. If you do not have questions and want to provide feedback on our chatbots, please visit our feedback page.

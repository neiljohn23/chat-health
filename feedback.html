Provide feedback

We welcome your feedback!

Thank you so much for filling out our feedback form! By giving us feedback on the different features of Chat Health, you’re helping us make it better. Please fill out the form below to help us gather more information on areas where we can improve. There is only one form for all three services provided - the Telegram bot, the text bot, and the Public Health Volunteer Program - please follow the instructions to ensure that you fill out the form accurately. It should take no more than 5 minutes to complete.

A note on data collection

This form does not collect your email addresses or ask for any identifiable information. Here’s how we will use the data you provide us:

  • We will use demographic information to get a better understanding of who is using our chatbot, and will help us figure out which demographics we can target better.
  • We will use response data for specific programs to improve each program and collect data on the areas that most need improvement.
  • We will use response data for specific programs to continually assess the impact of any improvements we make on new users.
